android-x86 sponsorship


Why?

This is an open source project. Like most other open source projects, we are volunteers that work without pay. Your support is one of the most important reasons why we're able continue working on it.
We receive a lot of requests such as :

  • Does Android-x86 support my xxx netbook?
  • Could you solve the yyy issue for my zzz tablet?

We cannot help, unless we have that device to debug and test. We are not rich enough to buy every device that the community asks us to support.
If you are a vendor that plans to ship or has already shipped products based on Android-x86, you should seriously consider making a donation. Because the more mature and successful our project becomes, the more better your products will be.

What?

We accept devices, money and developers.
Needless to say, we need more devices. We hope to support more wide range of devices. If you hope for us to be able to support your netbook/tablet, consider donating one. If you are a manufacturer, donate your products so that we can support them.

The project doesn't need much money to run. Most of the resources we use are free services provided by the open source community or sponsors, except the DNS registration. However, money donation is helpful. At least it makes us happier, even one dollar. We can buy some beers. :-)

If you are a vendor working on Android-x86 based products, ask your developers to work with us, and contribute back your bug fixes and improvements.

Who?

The project is founded by Chih-Wei Huang (cwhuang, in Taiwan) and Yi Sun (beyounn, in U.S.). We have developers from France, England, America, Japan, China Mainland and Korea, etc. See credits for more information regarding who's involved.
